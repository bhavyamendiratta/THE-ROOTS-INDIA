Theatre is intended to convey a message. It is meant for education. Similarly, The Roots India always strives to deliver relevant content. Our vision is to make theatre accessible to everyone. One of the aspects of Theatre is acting and it is intimidating that in India acting seems to be limited to Bollywood only but our modules makes the actor capable of exploring not only Bollywood but also performing and visual arts. Our modules are designed dealing the intricacies of acting from body to speech, scene studies to direction, amongst others. Our modules are designed for people of different age groups from all walks of life with a universal passion for theatre.
